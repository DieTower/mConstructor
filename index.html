About this app

Whether you are an aspiring/independent movie director, or simply a curious film fan, this application will meet your needs. As a tool used to organize every detail of a movie, from actors participating in it to the budget used on the equipment, it is very handy to help directors as they will be able to organize and supervise every section of the film they are directing, as well as making sure they don't go over the budget.

Make your movies!

Additional Features

Users who create an account have access to the tool that allows them to organize all the details of the movie they plan to direct. This includes the script, the script writers, the directors, the producers, the actors and the character that they play, the set designers, the makeup crew, the special effects crew, the camera crew, and any other people necessary for the creation of a movie, as well as the equipment used for each, such as costumes, cameras and microphones.
